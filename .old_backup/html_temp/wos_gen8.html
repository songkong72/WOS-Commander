Generation 8 Heroes

Hendrick, Sonya, and Gatot are Generation 8 heroes available in Whiteout Survival. These heroes become accessible when your state reaches an age of approximately 520 days.

Hendrick

Hendrick is a marksman hero of the Gen 8 heroes. He is a pirate with an Octopus sitting on his head. Being a marksman, he shoots his target with a gun from a distance.
